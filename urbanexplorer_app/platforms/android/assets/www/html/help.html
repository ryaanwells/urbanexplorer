Help Section

How do I use urbanexplorer?

You can use urbanexplorer any time you travel outdoors - just launch the app, pick a route and off you go! The app tracks the distance you travel and awards you medals for completing the route in a certain time.

How do I get medals?

You earn a medal for each route you complete. Each route has several stages - a route is completed when you complete all the stages. Complete the stages faster to bring down the overall route time and get better medals!

What happens if I can't complete a route before I finish exercising?

Don't worry! The app knows where you left off so you can pick right up from where you left off :)

How do you track my distance?

The app uses your GPS location to track your distance. You need an active GPS connection and a data plan to use this app!

What do you use my GPS location for?

We only hold onto your last known GPS location to work out how far you've travelled since the last time the app told us where you are. Nothing more!

How much data will urbanexplorer use?

Not much! Every effort has been made to minimise the amount of data required. When you're out running the app checks in with us about once a minute using your data plan. While every effort has been made to minimise the data transfer, you should be aware when you are reaching your data limits - we are not responsible for any charges incurred when using this app!
